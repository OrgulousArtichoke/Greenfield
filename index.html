Sumo Tomato

Sumo Tomato is a realtime multiplayer game we built using Node, express, Socketio, and the Phaser Javascript game library. We built this game because we wanted to learn about these new technologies and craft a fun experience people could share with their friends.

What you need

You'll need three things to play Sumo Tomato:

  1. A computer (main game screen)

  2. A phone

  3. Some friends!

Point the main display to http://hr3232.elasticbeanstalk.com and point the controllers to http://hr3232.elasticbeanstalk.com/controller. Press the READY button and when your pals are ready too your phones will automagically transform into Sumo controllers

How to Play

The premise of Sumo Tomato is decidedly high-concept: you're one of 4 Sumo Wrestlers who have given up the Dohy≈ç and decided to throw tomatoes at each other. Jump, tomato-chuck, and wrestle your way to victory-- the last Sumo standing wins!

We hope you have as much fun playing it as we did building it!
