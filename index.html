Sumo Tomato

Sumo Tomato is a realtime multiplayer game we built using Node, express, Socketio, and the Phaser Javascript game library. We built this game to experiment with these technologies and craft a fun experience that people could share with their friends.

What you need

You'll need three things to play Sumo Tomato:

  1. A computer (main game screen)

  2. A phone

  3. Some friends!

To get started, just point the main display to http://hr3232.elasticbeanstalk.com and then on your phones go to http://hr3232.elasticbeanstalk.com/controller. Tap the READY button and when everyone is ready to go your phones will automagically transform into Sumo controllers!

How to Play

The premise of Sumo Tomato is decidedly high-concept: you're one of 4 Sumo Wrestlers who have given up the Dohy≈ç and decided to throw tomatoes at each other. Jump, tomato-chuck, and wrestle your way to victory-- the last Sumo standing wins!

We hope you have as much fun playing it as we did building it!
